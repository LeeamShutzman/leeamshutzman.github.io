Systems Analysis Prototype

Github Repository   

What is it?

This prototype is the result of a semester long project for the Systems Analysis class that I took. The goal was to convert my university's paper ticketing system to a computerized one. It involved requirements gathering from employees using the current system, weekly progress reports, and producing a prototype among other deliverables. Our team was voted to have the most well done project among two classes with several teams in each.

How does it work?

The solution my team came up with was using QR codes instead of hang-tags with identification numbers. This would allow the public safety staff to scan the QR code and give a ticket through a tablet interface instead of writing the information. There is also a search feature where the ticketing officer could search for the car by it's features (license plate for example) in the case that it didn't have a QR code. This system would have solved many of the problems that were explained to us by the head of public saftey. Among these were digitizing records of tickets and speeding up the process of giving tickets.
